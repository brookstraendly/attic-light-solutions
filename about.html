A Note from the Founder

Why I started this.

I spent 15+ years in business operations — scaling platforms, building processes from scratch, and figuring out how to make small teams punch way above their weight. Along the way, I helped grow a company from 25 to 2,000+ users and drove over $3.5M in revenue.

I didn't learn AI from a textbook. I learned it by solving the same operational problems you're facing right now — too many manual tasks, tools that don't connect, teams drowning in busywork instead of doing their best work.

When I discovered what AI could actually do for day-to-day operations (not the sci-fi stuff, the practical stuff), I rebuilt my entire workflow. Then I started doing it for others.

Attic Light Solutions is the result: a consulting practice built on the belief that AI should make your work life simpler, not more complicated.

I lead every engagement personally, drawing from a trusted network of AI engineers, automation architects, workflow designers, and domain experts I've built over 15 years in the field. They plug in as needed, matched to your specific problem — no bloat, no benchwarming. You get one point of contact with senior-level attention, backed by the people who actually move the needle.

What I believe

  • AI should save you time on Monday, not next quarter
  • The best system is the one your team actually uses
  • Automation without understanding is just faster chaos
  • Every business deserves tools that work as hard as they do
